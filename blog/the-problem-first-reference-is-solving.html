🚧 The Problem First Reference Is Solving

1. Fake Profiles & Unreliable Candidates

Recruiters invest time and money on popular hiring platforms, only to receive:

  • Invalid or switched-off phone numbers
  • Uninterested or passive job seekers
  • Fake resumes or inflated experience
  • Drop-offs after initial screening

2. High Cost of Hiring

Job platforms charge ₹3,000+ per hire, but offer no guarantee of quality. For roles like delivery, telecalling, or sales, this becomes unsustainable — leading to wasted time, energy, and money.

3. Lack of Trust & Accountability

There's often no way to verify whether a candidate is:

  • Genuine
  • Actively looking
  • Suitable for the role

This results in bad hires, high attrition, and overburdened HR teams.

💡 The Idea Behind First Reference

"What if recruiters could get access to only verified, high-intent candidates — before wasting time on screening?"

That insight gave birth to First Reference:
A verified lead marketplace for recruiters, offering background-verified candidates with genuine job intent — at a fraction of the traditional hiring cost.

🚀 How First Reference Works (From Idea to Execution)

✅ 1. Data Collection – Powered by 1000+ BGV Partners

We've partnered with over 1,000 Background Verification (BGV) companies across India. These partners help us build a real-time pipeline of active, job-ready candidates.

In addition, we source leads via:

  • Internal surveys
  • Referral programs
  • Social media ads
  • Job-based campaign funnels

This enables First Reference to maintain a trustable, verified, and scalable lead pool.

✅ 2. Advanced Verification Process

Every candidate goes through a structured verification flow:

🔍 Phone Number Activation — AI Tool (Built In-House)

Our proprietary AI tool checks whether a candidate's phone number is active and reachable in real-time.

  • When a hiring requirement is submitted, the tool automatically validates every candidate.
  • This eliminates fake or unreachable leads, saving time for employers.

📌 Role Matching — Internal Smart Engine

We've developed an in-house Role Matching Tool that aligns candidate preferences with employer requirements.

  • It checks the candidate's desired role, intent level, and relevance.
  • Ensures the employer gets matching, high-intent candidates only.

🧾 Verification Includes:

  • Phone number validation
  • Role interest and intent screening
  • Employment & experience checks
  • Education & certification checks
  • Reference validation
  • Document capture (where available)

✅ 3. Segmented Lead Packs

Candidates are segmented by role and bundled into easy-to-use hiring packs, such as:

  • 🛵 Delivery Executives
  • 📞 Telecallers
  • 💼 Sales Executives

Each pack contains 100–200 verified candidates tailored to the employer's requirement.

✅ 4. Simple Pay-Per-Use Model

  • No subscriptions
  • No long-term lock-ins
  • Pricing starts at ₹399 per pack
  • Leads are shared via WhatsApp or email within 1 hour

This makes First Reference ideal for fast-paced hiring teams.

✅ 5. Human Support — No App Needed

Hiring managers get support via WhatsApp, call, or email.

  • Dedicated team to handle queries
  • Manual onboarding support
  • Google Forms used for smooth requirement collection

We've designed the process to work without needing an app, making it accessible for all recruiters — even small business owners.
